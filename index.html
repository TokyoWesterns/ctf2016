About

Tokyo Westerns/MMA CTF is a security competition hosted by MMA, tuat_mcc and Tokyo Westerns. The previous contest, MMA CTF 1st 2015, was held by only members of MMA. In this year, Tokyo Westerns have joined the contest organizers. Your task is to solve problems to get flags as many as possible.

Rules

  • This CTF is a team competition.
  • There is no limit on team size. Of course, you can participate as a single person team.
  • The flag format is "TWCTF{something}" unless otherwise specified.
  • You get points after submitting a flag to the score server.
  • Some challenges may have multiple flags.
  • If some teams have the same score, the team that submitted a flag earlier can be placed on a higher rank.
  • If you have any questions during the content, please contact us on IRC. (refer to "IRC Information" to get more details.)

Prohibitions

  • Do not attack/obstruct other teams.
  • Do not attack any other servers except *.chal.ctf.westerns.tokyo..
  • Do not share answers/hints with other teams.
  • Be kind to server resources. Too frequent access will be banned.
